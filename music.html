Music

If I had to say my favourite activity all time, it would probably be listening to music! Music is something I am very passionate about and have been listening to almost every day for 7 years. I've explored countless genres, musicians and eras and I sort of treat it like a hobby of mine. I love discovering new music and new sounds.

The last couple months I've been really into a lot of old Soul, R&B and funk from the 60s/70s! If you want some music recommendations, here are 5 songs below that I really enjoy!

  • Isaac Hayes - Medley: Ike's Rap III / Your Love Is So Doggone good

    One of the best soul recordings ever made!

  • Sly & Family Stone - Luv N' Haight

    Funk genius!

  • Teddy Pendergrass - Turn Off the Lights

    R&B classic!

  • Curtis Mayfield - Give Me Your Love (Love Song)

    From the soundtrack of Superfly (1972)! Classic!

  • Funkadelic - Red Hot Mama

    Badass!
